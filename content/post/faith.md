Faith in any form like religious faith or faith in ones own ability is not above reason. Unwavering faith is a sharp tool, indeed. Faith leads to belief. Belief destroys doubt. This paves the way to tireless pursuits---the kind of pursuits that resulted in the man-made wonders of the world.  In fact, some of the best minds invented it. Why? Both the simple minded and the intelligent feel and respond to pain. Faith is that thing that transcends this pain and fuses the person with the job in hand. Faith is an invented tool. When faith was taken to be above everything, genocides resulted. Faith needs to be in its place---its place as a tool. The intelligent mind created this tool after coming to terms with its own limits. It is consciousness that is superior to both! Now smile. :)
